About Us

Your trusted marathon travel experts

6 Majors is the world's leading specialist in travel for the Abbott World Marathon Majors. With over 15 years of experience, we've helped thousands of runners and supporters experience the world's greatest marathons.

Our team consists of former elite runners, travel industry veterans, and marathon enthusiasts who understand the unique demands of marathon travel. From securing elite bibs to organizing post-race celebrations, we handle every detail so you can focus on your run.
